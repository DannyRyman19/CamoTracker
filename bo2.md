# BO2 Black Ops 2 Camo Tracker

Track your Gold and Diamond camo progress for every weapon in Black Ops 2 multiplayer.

**Please Note:** This app does not automatically fetch your camo progression as it is impossible. You will have to enter your progression manually!

## Features

- Track all 14 camo challenges for every weapon across all 8 weapon classes
- See exactly how close you are to Gold on each individual weapon
- Diamond tracker — know which classes are one Gold away from Diamond
- Stats dashboard with overall completion percentage
- Built with iOS Liquid Glass design

## Weapon Classes

- **Assault Rifles** (8 weapons) — M8A1, MTAR, Type 25, SWAT-556, AN-94, SMR, FAL OSW, M27
- **SMGs** (6 weapons) — MP7, PDW-57, Vector K10, MSMC, Chicom CQB, Skorpion EVO
- **LMGs** (4 weapons) — LSAT, QBB LSW, HAMR, MK48
- **Shotguns** (4 weapons) — R870 MCS, Saiga 12, KSG, SRM 1216
- **Sniper Rifles** (4 weapons) — SVU-AS, DSR 50, Ballista, AS50
- **Pistols** (5 weapons) — B23R, Five Seven, KAP-40, Tac-45, Executioner
- **Launchers** (3 weapons) — SMAW, FHJ-18 AA, RPG-7
- **Specials** (4 weapons) — Combat Knife, Ballistic Knife, Crossbow, Assault Shield

## Camo Progression (14 challenges per weapon)

Each weapon has 14 camo challenges to complete before unlocking **Gold**. Earn Gold on every weapon in a class to unlock **Diamond**.

| # | Camo | Challenge Type |
|---|------|---------------|
| 1 | Devgru | Primary stat (headshots / one-shot kills / aircraft) |
| 2 | A-TACS AU | Primary stat |
| 3 | ERDL | Primary stat |
| 4 | Siberia | Primary stat |
| 5 | Choco | Primary stat |
| 6 | Blue Tiger | Primary stat |
| 7 | Bloodshot | Primary stat |
| 8 | Ghostex Delta 6 | Primary stat |
| 9 | Kryptek Typhon | Primary stat |
| 10 | Carbon Fiber | Longshot / Revenge medals |
| 11 | Cherry Blossom | Kills without attachments |
| 12 | Art of War | Kills without perks |
| 13 | Ronin | Double kill medals |
| 14 | Skulls | Bloodthirsty medals |
| ★ | **Gold** | Complete all 14 challenges |
| ◆ | **Diamond** | Gold on all weapons in class |
