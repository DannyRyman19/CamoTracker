# BO2 Black Ops 2 Camo Tracker
Please Note: This app does not automatically fetch your camo progression as it is impossible. You will have to enter your progression manually!

Call of Duty Black Ops 2 BO2 Camo Tracker assists with unlocking Gold and Diamond camos on every weapon. Track your camo challenges, multiplayer challenges, reticle unlocks and overall completion stats — all in one place.

Track these mastery camos:

Gold: Complete all 14 camo challenges on a single weapon
Diamond: Earn Gold on every weapon in a class
Plus: Multiplayer Challenges across Scorestreaks, Assignments, Game Modes, Career and Prestige — and all Optic Reticle challenges for Reflex, ACOG, Hybrid and EOTech

Know every single challenge and unlock requirement for each weapon. Track your reticle kill counts and medal challenges per optic. Complete challenge categories across Weapons, Optics, Scorestreaks, Assignments, Game Modes, Career and Prestige — including Specialized Optics, Combat Efficiency, Equipment, Perks and Wildcards.

No adverts! Data stored on-device — no account required.
